# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection

We collect the following personal data: name, first name, email address, login data, and service usage information.  
This information is collected when you register, use the service, or communicate with us.

## Data Usage

The collected data is used to:

- Provide, operate, and improve the service
- Manage your account, subscriptions, and billing
- Communicate with you regarding updates, notifications, and support
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
- Prevent fraud and ensure security

## Data Retention

Your data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service and fulfill legal obligations.  
When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.  
Your personal data may be shared only with trusted service providers and subcontractors who assist us in operating the service and are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

## Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.  
However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- Access: request a copy of your data
- Rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- Deletion: request removal of your data, where applicable
- Restriction: limit the processing of your data
- Objection: object to data processing based on legitimate interests
- Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: **contact@track-dev-time.dev**.

## Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please contact us at: **contact@track-dev-time.dev**.  
We will respond to your request within the legal timeframes.
